Mary-Lou O'Connell

Bowen Therapist

BSc(Hons), BTPA, ITEC, CBS

My journey with The Bowen Technique started over 10 years ago.

An old school friend invited me along to a Bowen Technique taster session weekend, a therapy I had not heard of, but I was keen to learn something new so went along. I was so bowled over by its light touch and effectiveness, I signed up to complete the course there and then.

I have always had a keen interest in the human body, food and healthy living and have been studying it in one form or another since my teenage years. After 18 years working within the food industry in sales and marketing, I had a light bulb moment, and decided to follow what I really loved and enjoyed, so I left my job and set up my business as a Bowen Therapist.

I keep my skills up to date by attending regular Continuous Professional Development courses (CPD) including full human body dissections. These courses are a continuous reminder to me just how amazing our bodies are.

I am a full member of the BTPA (Bowen Therapy Professional Association) and the FHT (Federation of Holistic Therapists), which means I am fully insured and abide by formal codes of ethics and conduct. I am also qualified in Anatomy and Physiology and First Aid.
